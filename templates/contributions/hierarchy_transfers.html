About Hierarchy Transfers

info

Hierarchy transfers allow funds to move between adjacent levels in the church hierarchy: Mission ↔ Area ↔ District ↔ Branch. These are NOT contributions or income, but allocations of funds.

  • Transfers can only occur between adjacent levels
  • Transfers are NOT counted as contributions or income
  • Transfers do NOT generate commission
  • Transfers are excluded from tithe calculations
