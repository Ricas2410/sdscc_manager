About Opening Balances

info

Opening balances represent pre-system funds that existed before system adoption. They are one-time entries per contribution type and require Mission Admin approval.

  • Opening balances are for CASH only (no PAYABLE/RECEIVABLE)
  • They are excluded from income, tithe, and commission calculations
  • They are clearly labeled as "Opening Balance â€“ Pre-System Funds"
  • They are included in balance visibility
