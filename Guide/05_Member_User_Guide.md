## **Login & Getting Started**

### **How to Login**
1. Open your church management system URL or mobile app
2. Enter your **Member ID** (e.g., "MEM001")
3. Enter your **PIN** (default: `12345`)
4. Click **"Login"**

### **First Time Login**
If this is your first time:
1. **Default PIN**: `12345`
2. **You'll be prompted** to change your PIN for security
3. **Choose a new 5-digit PIN** that you'll remember
4. **Confirm your new PIN**
5. **Login again** with your new PIN

### **Forgot Your PIN?**
If you can't remember your PIN:
1. Click **"Forgot PIN?"** on login screen
2. **Enter your Member ID**
3. **Answer your security question** (if set up)
4. **Reset PIN** will be sent to your email/phone
5. **Contact your branch admin** if you need help

### **Security Tips**
- **Choose a memorable PIN** but not obvious (like 12345)
- **Don't share your PIN** with anyone
- **Log out** when using shared devices
- **Keep your contact info updated** for account recovery

## **Profile Management**

### **Viewing Your Profile**
See all your personal information:
1. Click **"My Profile"** in the sidebar
2. **Your Information Shows**:
   - **Personal Details**: Name, contact info, address
   - **Church Information**: Member ID, branch, join date
   - **Spiritual Journey**: Baptism date, spiritual milestones
   - **Group Involvement**: Ministries and departments
   - **Family Information**: Family members (if applicable)

### **Updating Your Information**
You can edit certain personal details:
1. Click **"Edit Profile"**
2. **Editable Fields**:
   - **Phone Number**: Update your contact number
   - **Email Address**: Current email for communications
   - **Residential Address**: Where you live
   - **Profile Picture**: Upload or change your photo
   - **Emergency Contact**: Who to contact in emergencies
   - **Profession**: Your current occupation
   - **Marital Status**: Update if recently married/divorced
   - **Date of Birth**: Your birth date

3. **Non-Editable Fields** (Admin only):
   - **Member ID**: Permanent identification
   - **Branch Assignment**: Your church home
   - **Baptism Records**: Spiritual milestones
   - **Financial Records**: Contribution history
   - **Staff/Pastor Status**: Leadership roles

### **Profile Picture**
Add a personal touch:
1. **Click "Upload Photo"**
2. **Choose a clear, recent photo** of yourself
3. **Crop and adjust** as needed
4. **Save Photo**
- **Recommended**: Professional, clear headshot
- **Size**: Square format works best
- **Purpose**: Helps other members recognize you

### **Emergency Contact**
Keep your emergency information current:
1. **Contact Name**: Who should we call in emergencies?
2. **Relationship**: How are they related to you?
3. **Phone Number**: Best contact number
4. **Address**: Where they live (if different from you)

## **Viewing Your Contributions**

### **Your Contribution History**
Track your faithful giving:
1. Click **"My Contributions"** in the sidebar
2. **Contribution Overview**:

   - **Contribution Types**: By category (tithe, offering, etc.)

### **Detailed Contribution Records**
See each contribution:

- **Type**: Tithe, offering, special fund, etc.
- **Amount**: How much you gave
- **Method**: How you gave (cash, mobile money, etc.)
- **Reference**: Transaction reference (if applicable)

### **Contribution Statements**
Get official records of your giving:
1. Click **"Download Statement"**

Different ways you can give:

- **Offering**: General giving beyond tithe
- **Special Projects**: Building funds, mission projects
- **Thanksgiving**: Gratitude giving for blessings
- **Welfare**: Supporting members in need
- **Funeral Support**: Helping families during loss

### **Giving Records & Tax**
- **Official Records**: Your statements are official giving records

## **Troubleshooting & Support**

### **Common Member Issues**

#### **Login Problems**
**Problem**: Can't login to your account
- **Check**: Member ID is correct (no spaces)
- **Check**: PIN is 5 digits
- **Solution**: Use "Forgot PIN" or contact branch admin

#### **Missing Information**
**Problem**: Your profile shows incorrect information
- **Check**: When you last updated your profile
- **Solution**: Edit your profile with correct information
- **Contact**: Branch admin for changes you can't make

#### **Contribution Questions**
**Problem**: Your contribution history is incorrect
- **Check**: Date range you're viewing
- **Solution**: Contact branch admin to verify records
- **Note**: Contributions may take 24-48 hours to appear

#### **Attendance Issues**
**Problem**: Your attendance record is wrong
- **Check**: Which services you attended
- **Solution**: Let your branch admin know about the error
- **Note**: Attendance is recorded by church staff

#### **App Problems**
**Problem**: Mobile app isn't working
- **Check**: Internet connection is active
- **Check**: App is updated to latest version
- **Solution**: Restart app or reinstall if needed

### **Getting Help**
#### **Contact Your Branch**
For most issues, contact your church office:
- **Branch Administrator**: For account and profile issues
- **Church Secretary**: For attendance and contribution questions
- **Pastor**: For spiritual care and counseling

#### **Technical Support**
For system technical problems:
- **Email**: tech-support@yourchurch.org
- **Phone**: [Church technical support number]
- **Help Desk**: Available during church office hours
- **Online Help**: Built-in help guides and FAQs

#### **What to Include When Asking for Help**
- **Your Name and Member ID**

- **What you've already tried**
- **Error messages** (if any)

#### **Stay Engaged**
