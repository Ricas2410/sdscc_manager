## **Introduction**

### **Who is a District Executive?**
As a **District Executive**, you are responsible for **overseeing multiple branches** within a geographical district. You serve as the **key link between Area leadership and local churches**, ensuring that all branches under your care operate effectively, maintain high standards, and fulfill the church's mission at the local level.

### **Your Core Responsibilities**
- **Branch Supervision**: Oversee all branches in your district
- **Financial Oversight**: Monitor and support branch financial health
- **Leadership Development**: Support and develop branch executives and pastors
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensure compliance with church standards and policies
- **Strategic Implementation**: Execute Area and Mission initiatives at the district level
- **Pastoral Support**: Ensure pastoral care coverage across the district

### **Your Scope of Authority**
‚úÖ **What You CAN Do** (for your district only):
- View all data from all branches in your district
- Monitor financial performance across the entire district
- Create district-wide announcements and events
- Generate district-level reports and analytics
- Support branch leaders and pastors
- Ensure compliance with church policies
- Coordinate district-wide activities and programs

‚ùå **What You CANNOT Do**:
- Access other districts' data
- Edit financial records (view-only)
- Modify system-wide settings
- Override branch-level decisions without proper process
- Access Area-level administrative functions

## **Troubleshooting & Support**

### **Common District Executive Issues**

#### **Branch Performance Problems**
**Problem**: Branch consistently underperforms
- **Analyze**: Root causes of poor performance
- **Intervene**: Provide targeted support and resources
- **Monitor**: Track improvement progress
- **Escalate**: Seek Area help if needed

#### **Financial Compliance Issues**
**Problem**: Multiple branches with late remittances
- **Investigate**: Understand reasons for delays
- **Communicate**: Clear expectations and consequences
- **Support**: Help branches solve payment problems
- **Enforce**: Apply compliance policies consistently

#### **Leadership Conflicts**
**Problem**: Disputes between executives or pastors
- **Mediate**: Facilitate conflict resolution
- **Counsel**: Provide biblical guidance and wisdom
- **Document**: Record interventions and outcomes
- **Refer**: Escalate serious issues to Area

#### **Pastoral Vacancies**
**Problem**: Branch without pastoral leadership
- **Assess**: Immediate pastoral needs
- **Arrange**: Temporary pastoral coverage
- **Recruit**: Search for permanent pastor
- **Support**: Help transition to new leadership
