## **Introduction**

### **Who is an Area Executive?**
As an **Area Executive**, you are responsible for **overseeing multiple districts** and their branches within a geographical area. You serve as the **bridge between Mission HQ and local churches**, ensuring that all districts under your care operate effectively and align with the church's vision and policies.

### **Your Core Responsibilities**
- **District Supervision**: Oversee all districts in your area
- **Financial Oversight**: Monitor financial health across all branches
- **Leadership Development**: Support and develop district and branch leaders
- **Strategic Implementation**: Execute Mission initiatives at the area level
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensure compliance with church standards and policies
- **Reporting**: Provide comprehensive area reports to Mission leadership

### **Your Scope of Authority**
‚úÖ **What You CAN Do** (for your area only):
- View all data from all districts and branches in your area
- Monitor financial performance across the entire area
- Create area-wide announcements and events
- Generate area-level reports and analytics
- Support district and branch leaders
- Ensure compliance with church policies
- Coordinate area-wide activities and programs

‚ùå **What You CANNOT Do**:
- Access other areas' data
- Edit financial records (view-only)
- Modify system-wide settings
- Override branch-level decisions
- Access Mission-level administrative functions
