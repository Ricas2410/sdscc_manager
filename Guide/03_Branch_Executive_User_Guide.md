## **Introduction**

### **Who is a Branch Executive?**
The **Branch Executive** is responsible for the **complete management of a single church branch**. You are the primary administrator for your branch's operations, finances, members, and activities.

### **Your Core Responsibilities**
- **Branch Operations**: Manage all day-to-day branch activities
- **Financial Management**: Record contributions, expenditures, and process remittances
- **Member Care**: Manage member registration, updates, and pastoral support
- **Attendance Tracking**: Monitor service attendance and participation
- **Reporting**: Generate branch reports for district/area oversight
- **Communication**: Create branch-level announcements and events

### **Your Access Level**
‚úÖ **What You CAN Do** (for your branch only):
- Add, edit, and view branch members
- Record and manage contributions
- Record and manage expenditures
- Track attendance
- Create branch announcements and events
- Generate branch reports
- Manage branch groups and departments
- Process remittances to Mission

‚ùå **What You CANNOT Do**:
- Access other branches' data
- Modify system-wide settings
- Manage users at other levels
- Access Mission-level financial data (except your remittances)
- Change contribution type rules
- View district/area-wide aggregated data

## **Contribution Management**

### **Understanding Contributions**
Contributions are the **financial lifeblood** of your branch. You'll record two main types:

#### **General Contributions**
- Collected from everyone (e.g., offerings, special funds)
- Recorded as totals, not per person
- Examples: Sunday offering, building fund, special projects

#### **Individual Contributions**
- Tracked per person (e.g., tithes, personal pledges)
- Recorded member-by-member
- Examples: Tithe, personal commitments, funeral contributions

### **Recording General Contributions**
1. Click **"Contributions"** ‚Üí **"General Contributions"**
2. **Select Date**: Choose the service date
3. **Enter Amounts** for each contribution type:
   - **Offering**: Total Sunday offering
   - **Thanksgiving**: Special thanksgiving giving
   - **Building Fund**: Building project contributions
   - **Welfare**: Welfare and support collections
   - **Other**: Additional contribution types

4. **Add Notes** (optional):
   - Service details
   - Special circumstances
   - Visitor contributions

5. **Review Totals**: System shows automatic calculations
6. Click **"Save Contributions"**

### **Recording Individual Contributions**
1. Click **"Contributions"** ‚Üí **"Individual Contributions"**
2. **Select Contribution Type**: e.g., Tithe
3. **Select Date**: Service date
4. **Enter Contributions** by member:
   - **Member List**: Shows all branch members
   - **Amount**: Enter amount given by each member
   - **Notes**: Special circumstances for specific members
   - **Filter**: Show only contributors or all members

5. **Auto-Save**: System saves as you type
6. **Submit**: Click to finalize and record

### **Funeral Contributions**
Special handling for funeral support:
1. Click **"Contributions"** ‚Üí **"Funeral Contributions"**
2. **Select Deceased**: Choose from list or add new
3. **Record Contributions**:
   - **Contributor**: Who gave
   - **Amount**: How much given
   - **Payment Type**: Full or partial payment
   - **Notes**: Relationship to deceased

4. **Track Outstanding**: System shows remaining balance
5. **Update as payments** come in

### **Contribution History & Reports**
1. Click **"Contributions"** ‚Üí **"Contribution History"**
2. **View and Filter**:
   - **Date Range**: Specific periods
   - **Contribution Type**: By category
   - **Member**: Individual member history
   - **Service Type**: Sunday, midweek, special

3. **Export Data**:
   - **Excel**: For detailed analysis
   - **PDF**: For presentations
   - **Member Statements**: Individual giving reports

## **Remittance Processing**

### **Understanding Remittances**
**Remittances** are your branch's **payments to Mission HQ** based on contribution splits. After collecting contributions, you must send Mission's portion according to established percentages.

### **Monthly Remittance Process**
#### **Step 1: Calculate Amount Owed**
1. Click **"Remittances"** ‚Üí **"Calculate Remittance"**
2. **Select Month**: Choose the month to process
3. **Review Calculation**:
   - **Total Contributions**: All contributions for the month
   - **Mission Percentage**: % that goes to HQ
   - **Amount Owed**: Final amount to send
   - **Branch Retention**: Amount that stays locally

#### **Step 2: Record Payment**
1. Click **"Remittances"** ‚Üí **"Record Payment"**
2. **Payment Details**:
   - **Amount**: How much you're sending
   - **Payment Date**: When payment was made
   - **Payment Method**: Bank transfer, mobile money, cash
   - **Reference Number**: Transaction reference
   - **Bank Account**: Which account you paid from/to

3. **Upload Evidence**:
   - **Bank Statement**: Screenshot or PDF
   - **Mobile Money Confirmation**: Screenshot
   - **Receipt Image**: Payment receipt photo

4. **Add Notes**:
   - Any payment issues
   - Special circumstances
   - Future payment arrangements

5. Click **"Submit Remittance"**

#### **Step 3: Mission Verification**
After submission:
- **Status**: Shows as "Sent" pending verification
- **Mission Review**: Mission Admin will verify your payment
- **Status Update**: Changes to "Verified" when approved
- **Issues**: Mission may return for clarification if needed

### **Remittance History & Status**
1. Click **"Remittances"** ‚Üí **"History"**
2. **View All Remittances**:
   - **Month**: Which month each payment covers
   - **Amount**: How much was sent
   - **Status**: Sent, Verified, Pending, Issue
   - **Date**: When payment was recorded
   - **Reference**: Transaction details

3. **Filter by**:
   - Date range
   - Payment status
   - Payment method

### **Remittance Best Practices**
#### **Timely Payment**
- **Pay by 5th** of each month for previous month
- **Avoid late payments** to maintain good standing
- **Communicate early** if payment will be delayed

#### **Accurate Recording**
- **Double-check amounts** before submission
- **Upload clear evidence** of payment
- **Keep payment receipts** for your records

#### **Problem Resolution**
- **Contact Mission Admin** quickly if issues arise
- **Provide additional documentation** when requested
- **Maintain communication** during verification process

## **Monthly Closing Process**

### **Understanding Monthly Closing**
Monthly closing **freezes the financial data** for a month and:
- Locks contribution and expenditure entries
- Calculates commission eligibility
- Generates required reports
- Triggers remittance requirements

### **Monthly Closing Steps**
#### **Step 1: Pre-Closing Checklist**
Before closing the month, verify:
- **All contributions entered**: No missing contribution entries
- **All expenditures recorded**: All expenses are in the system
- **Bank reconciliations complete**: System matches bank statements
- **Member issues resolved**: Any member concerns addressed

#### **Step 2: Review Monthly Data**
1. Click **"Financial"** ‚Üí **"Monthly Review"**
2. **Review Contributions**:
   - **Total amounts** look correct
   - **No missing dates** or services
   - **Proper categorization** of all contributions
   - **Individual contributions** are complete

3. **Review Expenditures**:
   - **All receipts uploaded**
   - **Proper categorization** of expenses
   - **Approval documentation** complete
   - **Budget compliance** reviewed

#### **Step 3: Generate Monthly Report**
1. Click **"Reports"** ‚Üí **"Monthly Report"**
2. **Review Report Sections**:
   - **Financial Summary**: Income and expenses
   - **Membership Changes**: New and inactive members
   - **Attendance Statistics**: Service attendance data
   - **Program Activities**: Events and activities held

3. **Add Notes**:
   - **Special circumstances** this month
   - **Challenges faced** and how resolved
   - **Plans for next month**
   - **Prayer requests** and praises

#### **Step 4: Submit for Review**
1. **Review all data** one final time
2. Click **"Submit Monthly Report"**
3. **Wait for district/area review**:
   - They may ask for clarification
   - They may request additional information
   - They will approve or request changes

#### **Step 5: Process Remittance**
After report approval:
1. **Calculate remittance** amount owed
2. **Make payment** to Mission HQ
3. **Record payment** in system
4. **Upload payment evidence**
5. **Wait for Mission verification**

### **Monthly Closing Best Practices**
#### **Timeline**
- **Last day of month**: Complete all data entry
- **1st-3rd**: Review and generate reports
- **4th-5th**: Submit reports and process remittance
- **5th-10th**: District/area review and approval

#### **Data Quality**
- **Double-check all entries** for accuracy
- **Upload all receipts** and documentation
- **Resolve member questions** before closing
- **Maintain backup copies** of reports

## **Pastor Commission Management**

### **Understanding Pastor Commissions**
Pastors may receive **commission based on tithe collection** when the branch meets its monthly tithe target. As Branch Executive, you help track and report this.

### **Commission Eligibility Rules**
- **Tithe Target**: Monthly goal set by Mission
- **Achievement Required**: Must meet or exceed target
- **Commission Rate**: Percentage of tithe collected (usually 10%)
- **Eligible Recipients**: Pastor(s) designated by Mission

### **Tracking Tithe Progress**
1. Click **"Contributions"** ‚Üí **"Tithe Tracking"**
2. **Monthly View**:
   - **Monthly Target**: Goal for this month
   - **Current Collected**: Amount collected so far
   - **Progress Percentage**: How close to target
   - **Remaining Needed**: Amount still needed

3. **Commission Projection**:
   - **If Target Met**: Estimated commission amount
   - **Current Commission**: Commission on collected amount
   - **Shortfall Impact**: What's lost if target not met

### **Commission Reporting**
1. **Monthly Calculation**:
   - System automatically calculates at month-end
   - Based on actual tithe collected
   - Applies commission percentage

2. **Mission Approval**:
   - Mission Admin reviews and approves
   - May adjust based on special circumstances
   - Final approval required for payment

3. **Payment Processing**:
   - Mission handles commission payments
   - Pastor receives payment directly
   - You receive confirmation of payment

### **Supporting Pastor Success**
#### **Encouraging Tithe Faithfulness**
- **Regular teaching** on tithing principles
- **Testimony sharing** of tithing blessings
- **Progress updates** to the congregation
- **Celebration** when targets are met

#### **Tracking and Reporting**
- **Weekly updates** on tithe progress
- **Transparent communication** with pastor
- **Accurate record-keeping** of all tithes
- **Timely reporting** to Mission

## **Troubleshooting & Support**

### **Common Branch Executive Issues**

#### **Financial Discrepancies**
**Problem**: Contribution totals don't match cash counts
- **Check**: All contribution types entered
- **Check**: Individual vs general contributions
- **Solution**: Review service date contributions

#### **Member Access Issues**
**Problem**: Member cannot login or reset PIN
- **Check**: Member ID is correct
- **Check**: Member account is active
- **Solution**: Reset member PIN to `12345`

#### **Report Generation Problems**
**Problem**: Reports show incorrect data
- **Check**: Date range filters
- **Check**: Include/exclude settings
- **Solution**: Verify fiscal year boundaries

#### **Remittance Calculation Issues**
**Problem**: Amount owed to Mission seems wrong
- **Check**: Contribution split percentages
- **Check**: Date range for calculation
- **Solution**: Verify with Mission Admin

### **Getting Help**
#### **When to Contact District/Area**
- **Report approval delays**
- **Policy clarification needed**
- **Unusual financial situations**
- **System access problems**

#### **When to Contact Mission HQ**
- **Remittance verification issues**
- **System technical problems**
- **Contribution type questions
- **Emergency financial situations**

#### **Information to Provide**
- **Your Branch Name and Code**
- **Exact Issue Description**
- **Steps You've Already Taken**
