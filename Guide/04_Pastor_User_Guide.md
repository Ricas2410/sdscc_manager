## **Introduction**

### **Who is a Pastor in SDSCC?**
As a **Pastor**, you are a **spiritual leader and staff member** with unique access to support your ministry. Your role combines spiritual care with limited administrative functions to help you serve your congregation effectively.

### **Your Core Responsibilities**
- **Spiritual Leadership**: Shepherd and care for your congregation
- **Member Pastoral Care**: Track and support member spiritual journeys
- **Teaching & Preaching**: Manage sermons and spiritual content
- **Attendance Oversight**: Monitor congregational engagement
- **Communication**: Create announcements and events for your flock
- **Financial Awareness**: View (but not edit) financial data for informed leadership

### **Your Access Level**
‚úÖ **What You CAN Do**:
- View and edit member information (non-financial)
- Manage attendance records
- Create sermons and spiritual content
- Make announcements and events (based on your pastoral rank)
- View financial data (read-only)
- Track your commission and payroll
- Add pastoral notes and counseling records
- Manage groups and ministries

‚ùå **What You CANNOT Do** (unless also assigned as Branch Admin):
- Enter or edit contributions
- Enter or edit expenditures
- Process remittances
- Change system settings
- Access other branches' data
- Edit financial records

### **How to Login**
1. Open your church management system URL
2. Enter your **Member ID** (e.g., "PST001")

## **Pastoral Roles & Access Levels**

### **Understanding Pastoral Ranks**
Your access varies based on your pastoral rank:

#### **üè° Branch Pastor**
- **Scope**: Single branch congregation
- **Can Create**: Branch-level announcements and events
- **Financial View**: Branch financial data only
- **Member Access**: Branch members only

#### **üè¢ District Pastor**
- **Scope**: Multiple branches in a district
- **Can Create**: District-wide announcements and events
- **Financial View**: All branches in district (read-only)
- **Member Access**: All district members

#### **üåç Area Pastor**
- **Scope**: Multiple districts in an area
- **Can Create**: Area-wide announcements and events
- **Financial View**: All branches in area (read-only)
- **Member Access**: All area members

#### **üèõÔ∏è Mission Pastor**
- **Scope**: Entire organization
- **Can Create**: Mission-wide announcements and events
- **Financial View**: All branches (read-only)
- **Member Access**: All members organization-wide

#### **üëë General Overseer**
- **Scope**: Entire organization with highest authority
- **Can Create**: Any level announcements and events
- **Financial View**: Complete organization view
- **Member Access**: All members

### **Dual Role: Pastor + Administrator**
If you're also assigned as a **Branch/District/Area Admin**, you get additional permissions:
- **Contribution Entry**: Can record contributions
- **Expenditure Entry**: Can record expenses
- **Administrative Functions**: Full admin capabilities for your level
- **Financial Editing**: Can modify financial records

## **Member Care & Management**

### **Viewing Member Profiles**
Access comprehensive member information:
1. Click **"Members"** ‚Üí **"Member Directory"**
2. **Find members** using:
   - **Search**: By name, member ID, phone
   - **Filters**: By department, baptism status, activity level
3. **Member Profile Shows**:
   - **Personal Information**: Contact details, family info
   - **Spiritual Journey**: Baptism date, spiritual milestones
   - **Involvement**: Departments, ministries, roles
   - **Giving History**: Contribution patterns (read-only)
   - **Attendance Record**: Service participation
   - **Pastoral Notes**: Your counseling records

### **Editing Member Information**
You can update **non-financial member details**:
1. Find and click on the member
2. Click **"Edit Member"**
3. **Editable Fields**:
   - **Phone Number**: Updated contact information
   - **Email Address**: Current email
   - **Residential Address**: Where they live
   - **Emergency Contact**: Who to contact in emergencies
   - **Profession**: Current occupation
   - **Marital Status**: Marriage information
   - **Active Status**: Mark as active/inactive

4. **Non-Editable Fields** (Admin only):
   - **Member ID**: Permanent identification
   - **Branch Assignment**: Church membership
   - **Financial Records**: Contribution history
   - **Staff Status**: Employment information

### **Pastoral Notes & Counseling**
Record your pastoral care interactions:
1. Click **"Pastoral Care"** ‚Üí **"Add Pastoral Note"**
2. **Counseling Session Details**:
   - **Member**: Who you met with
   - **Date**: When the session occurred
   - **Session Type**: Counseling, visitation, prayer, etc.
   - **Topic**: Main issue discussed
   - **Notes**: Detailed conversation notes
   - **Follow-up Required**: Next steps needed
   - **Confidentiality Level**: Who can view this note

3. **Save Pastoral Note**

### **Member Spiritual Journey Tracking**
Track spiritual development:
1. **Baptism Records**: Date, location, officiating minister
2. **Spiritual Milestones**: Important spiritual events
3. **Ministry Involvement**: Areas of service
4. **Leadership Development**: Leadership roles and training
5. **Spiritual Gifts**: Identified gifts and talents

## **Attendance Management**

### **Recording Service Attendance**
Track who's attending your services:
1. Click **"Attendance"** ‚Üí **"Mark Attendance"**
2. **Service Details**:
   - **Service Type**: Sunday, Midweek, Youth, etc.
   - **Date**: Service date
   - **Sermon Title**: Main message title
   - **Preacher**: Who delivered the message (you or guest)

3. **Attendance Counts**:
   - **Total Attendance**: Everyone present
   - **Adults**: Adult members present
   - **Youth**: Young people present
   - **Children**: Children present
   - **Visitors**: First-time guests
   - **Regular Members**: Active members present

4. **Individual Attendance** (Optional but Recommended):
   - **Mark each member** present/absent
   - **Add notes** about specific members
   - **Record visitor information** for follow-up

5. **Save Attendance**

### **Visitor Follow-Up**
Care for first-time guests:
1. During attendance, click **"Add Visitor"**
2. **Visitor Information**:
   - **Name**: Full name
   - **Phone**: Contact number
   - **Address**: Where they live
   - **How They Heard**: How they found your church
   - **Who Invited Them**: Member who brought them

3. **Follow-Up Planning**:
   - **Assign Follow-Up**: Who will contact them
   - **Follow-Up Date**: When to reach out
   - **Special Notes**: Interests, needs, prayer requests
   - **Welcome Gift**: Plan for welcome package

### **Attendance Analytics & Insights**
Understand congregation patterns:
1. Click **"Attendance"** ‚Üí **"Analytics"**
2. **Key Metrics**:
   - **Average Attendance**: Typical service size
   - **Growth Trends**: Month-over-month changes
   - **Member Engagement**: How often members attend
   - **Visitor Retention**: How many visitors return
   - **Service Type Comparison**: Which services draw best

3. **Pastoral Insights**:
   - **Declining Attendance**: Members needing outreach
   - **Newcomer Patterns**: How guests are finding you
   - **Seasonal Variations**: Attendance throughout the year
   - **Ministry Effectiveness**: Program impact on attendance

## **Commission & Payroll**

### **Pastor Commission System**
If you're eligible for commission based on tithe performance:
1. Click **"My Commission"** in sidebar
2. **Commission Dashboard**:
   - **Monthly Target**: Tithe goal for your branch
   - **Current Progress**: How much has been collected
   - **Achievement Percentage**: Progress toward goal
   - **Commission Amount**: What you'll earn if target met
   - **Commission Status**: Pending, Approved, Paid

3. **Commission History**:
   - **Monthly Breakdown**: Each month's performance
   - **Payment History**: When commissions were paid
   - **Target Achievement**: Your track record

### **Commission Eligibility Rules**
- **Tithe Target**: Must meet or exceed monthly goal
- **Commission Rate**: Usually 10% of tithe collected
- **Mission Approval**: Final approval required from Mission Admin
- **Payment Processing**: Handled by Mission HQ

### **Payroll Information**
If you're on Mission payroll:
1. Click **"My Payroll"** in sidebar
2. **Payroll Dashboard**:
   - **Salary Amount**: Your monthly salary
   - **Allowances**: Additional payments (housing, transport)
   - **Deductions**: Taxes, insurance, other deductions
   - **Net Pay**: Take-home amount
   - **Payment Status**: Paid/Pending for current month

3. **Pay Slips**:
   - **Download PDF**: Official pay slip each month
   - **Payment History**: All past payments
   - **Year-to-Date Totals**: Cumulative earnings

4. **Tax Documents**:
   - **Annual Statements**: For tax purposes
   - **Payment Summaries**: Official payment records

## **Troubleshooting & Support**

### **Common Pastor Issues**

#### **Member Access Problems**
**Problem**: Member can't login or access their profile
- **Check**: Member ID is correct
- **Check**: Member account is active
- **Solution**: Reset member PIN to `12345`

#### **Attendance Recording Issues**
**Problem**: Can't find member in attendance list
- **Check**: Member is assigned to your branch
- **Check**: Member is marked as active
- **Solution**: Update member status or assignment

#### **Sermon Upload Problems**
**Problem**: Sermon file won't upload
- **Check**: File size is within limits
- **Check**: File format is supported
- **Solution**: Convert file or reduce file size

#### **Commission Questions**
**Problem**: Commission amount seems incorrect
- **Check**: Monthly tithe target and collection
- **Check**: Commission percentage settings
- **Solution**: Contact Mission Admin for clarification

### **Getting Help**
#### **When to Contact Branch Admin**
- **Technical issues** with system access

- **Facility scheduling conflicts**
- **Equipment needs**

#### **When to Contact District/Area Leadership**

- **Inter-church coordination**

#### **When to Contact Mission HQ**
- **System-wide technical problems**
- **Commission and payroll issues**
