## **Introduction**

### **Who is an Auditor?**
The **Auditor** (also known as **Board of Trustees** member) is responsible for **independent financial oversight**, **compliance verification**, and **internal control** across the entire SDSCC church system.

### **Your Core Responsibilities**
- **Full Financial Visibility**: See all financial data across all branches and levels
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Ensure adherence to financial policies and procedures
- **Fraud Prevention**: Detect and investigate suspicious financial activities
- **Audit Trail Review**: Monitor all system changes and transactions
- **Report Generation**: Create comprehensive audit reports for leadership
- **Verification**: Confirm accuracy of financial records and remittances

### **Your Access Level**
âœ… **What You CAN See**:
- All contributions from all branches
- All expenditures (branch + mission level)
- All remittance records and verifications
- All audit logs and system changes
- All payroll records (if enabled by Mission Admin)
- All member information (read-only)
- All reports and analytics

âŒ **What You CANNOT Do**:
- Add, edit, or delete any financial records
- Modify user accounts or permissions
- Change system settings
- Approve or reject transactions (only verify and report)
- Access configuration settings

## **Remittance Verification**

### **Understanding Remittances**
**Remittances** are payments branches make to Mission HQ based on contribution splits. Your role is to **verify** these payments are correct and properly recorded.

### **Remittance Tracking Dashboard**
1. Click **"Remittance Tracking"**
2. You'll see:
   - **All Branches**: Remittance status for each
   - **Monthly View**: Current and previous months
   - **Payment Status**: Sent, Verified, Pending, Overdue
   - **Amount Details**: Owed, sent, verified

### **Remittance Verification Process**
#### **Step 1: Review Expected Amounts**
For each branch, verify:
- **Calculated Amount**: System calculation based on splits
- **Expected Timeline**: When payment should be made
- **Payment Method**: How branch should pay

#### **Step 2: Verify Payment Evidence**
When branch marks as "Sent":
- **Check Payment Proof**: Bank statements, mobile money screenshots
- **Verify Amount**: Matches expected amount
- **Confirm Date**: Within expected timeframe
- **Check Reference**: Transaction reference is valid

#### **Step 3: Track Verification Status**
- **Pending**: Awaiting your review
- **Verified**: You've confirmed payment
- **Rejected**: Payment issues (with reason)
- **Overdue**: Late beyond grace period

### **Remittance Compliance Reports**
#### **Monthly Compliance Report**
Shows:
- **On-Time Payments**: Branches paying on schedule
- **Late Payments**: Branches with delays
- **Missing Payments**: Branches that haven't paid
- **Payment Patterns**: Historical compliance trends

#### **Variance Analysis**
Compare:
- **Expected vs Actual**: Differences in amounts
- **Timing Variance**: Early/late payment patterns
- **Method Consistency**: Payment method changes

### **Types of Audit Flags**
#### **ðŸ”´ High Priority Flags**
- **Missing Remittances**: Branches overdue on payments
- **Unusual Transaction Patterns**: Suspicious financial activity
- **Policy Violations**: Breaking established rules
- **Security Breaches**: Unauthorized access attempts

#### **ðŸŸ¡ Medium Priority Flags**
- **Late Entries**: Contributions recorded after closing
- **Missing Documentation**: Expenses without receipts
- **Data Inconsistencies**: Mismatched calculations
- **Compliance Gaps**: Minor policy deviations

#### **ðŸŸ¢ Low Priority Flags**

- **Data Quality**: Minor data problems
- **Performance Issues**: Slow operations

### **Managing Audit Flags**
1. Click **"Audit Flags"** in sidebar
2. Review each flag:
   - **Flag Details**: What triggered the alert
   - **Severity**: Priority level
   - **Affected Branch/Area**: Location of issue
   - **Recommended Action**: Suggested resolution

3. **Take Action**:
   - **Investigate**: Look into the issue
   - **Resolve**: Mark as addressed
   - **Escalate**: Send to Mission Admin if serious
   - **Monitor**: Watch for recurrence

### **Custom Alert Rules**
You can configure custom alerts for:
- **Amount Thresholds**: Flag transactions above certain amounts
- **Frequency Limits**: Too many transactions in short time
- **Time-based Rules**: Activities outside normal hours
- **User Patterns**: Unusual user behavior

## **Fraud Detection Tools**

### **Built-in Fraud Detection**
The system includes automatic fraud detection mechanisms:

#### **ðŸ” Anomaly Detection**
- **Unusual Amounts**: Transactions outside normal ranges
- **Strange Patterns**: Activities that don't match historical patterns
- **Time Anomalies**: Activities at odd times
- **Location Issues**: Activities from unusual locations

#### **ðŸ“Š Pattern Analysis**
- **Duplicate Detection**: Similar transactions that might be duplicates
- **Round Number Analysis**: Unusual round-number transactions
- **Frequency Analysis**: Too many transactions from same source
- **Sequence Analysis**: Transactions in suspicious sequences

### **Manual Fraud Investigation**
When you suspect fraud:
1. **Isolate the Issue**: Identify specific transactions or users
2. **Gather Evidence**: Collect all relevant data
3. **Analyze Patterns**: Look for broader schemes
4. **Document Findings**: Create detailed investigation report
5. **Recommend Action**: Suggest next steps to leadership

### **Red Flag Indicators**
Watch for:
- **Consistent Round Numbers**: Always round amounts (e.g., $100.00)
- **Same-Day Patterns**: Multiple similar transactions same day
- **After-Hours Activity**: Transactions outside business hours
- **Rapid Corrections**: Quick edits after original entry
- **Missing Documentation**: Incomplete supporting evidence
